# RFM-Analysis-on-an-UK-based-online-retail-store
Customer segmentation using RFM analysis on retail data to identify high-value and at-risk customers,  and provide insights to improve retention, engagement, and business growth.
Analysed 500K+ transactions and segmented 4K+ customers using RFM methodology
. Built an RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) model to segment customers into actionable groups such as Champions, Loyal, and At-Risk
. At Risk Customers consisted of around 17 percent and their revenue contribution was around 10 percent of the total revenue. This group of customers are the group which needs most attention and company must use its resources to retain them. Also, the average retency of the group was found to be 135 days which shows hope of them coming back with better offers.
. Observed that Champions and Loyal customers, consisting of 43% of the base, generated a majority share of revenue of 82%, emphasizing their business importance for the company.
. Observed that high-frequency customers generated 2x–3x higher revenue, reinforcing the importance of engagement-driven strategies
